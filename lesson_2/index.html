PRESENT SIMPLE
ТЕПЕРІШНІЙ ЗВИЧАЙНИЙ ПРОСТИЙ ЧАС

It`s meme about me

  • Rules або коли використовуємо

    1. Постійна, регулярна, повторювана дія (I wake up at 6:15 a.m)
    2. Закони природи (The plants grow better in the sun.)
    3. Розклад - уроків, поїздів, тощо (The train leaves at 3pm.)
    4. LOVE, LIKE, NEED, HATE, WANT, BELIEVE, PREFERE… - ONLY SIMPLE TENSES (I want a coffee.) Non-continuous Verbs
    5. Спортивні коментарі (He takes the ball, he runs down the field, and he scores!)

  • Markers або Слова маркери часу Pr.Simple

    1. Every day, every year, every oes (time) В КІНЦІ
    2. Always, usually, often, sometimes, hardly ever, never ПІСЛЯ ІМЕННИКА

  • Construction або Конструкція речення в Pr.Simple

    Examples

    • +Cats like milk.
    • -They don't run every day.
    • ?Do they visit you?
    • + I drink coffee ed. She drinkS coffee ed.
    • - I don’t drink tea ed. She doesn’t drink coffee ed.
    • ? Do you drink coffee ed? Does he drink coffee ed

  • To be or Auxiliary verbs

    HE, SHE, IT - does / I, WE, YOU, THEY - do

    Examples

    • Does --He doesn't look like a brother. She doesn’t run away from problems.
    • Do --Do you study? Parents don’t live with us.
